Annual Civic Engagement & Tree Planting Drive

Our community events are expanding rapidly! This summer, the city council is organizing multiple tree-planting campaigns across all public parks. Residents of all ages are welcome to join. Tools and snacks will be provided by local sponsors. It is a fantastic opportunity to meet your neighbors and improve the environment.

In other news, our local tech incubator is offering free coding courses for high-school students. The bootcamp starts early July at the city library. Seats are limited to 30, and registrations are handled strictly on a first-come, first-served basis. Sign up in the portal today!
